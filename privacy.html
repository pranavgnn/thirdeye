Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 22 Oct 2025

Owner

Traffic Violation Analyser

Contact

Contact the owner using the details provided in the application or via the associated repository.

What We Collect

  • Images you send via WhatsApp for analysis
  • WhatsApp identifiers (phone number/wa_id)
  • Model outputs (violation summary, matched violations, license plate text if detected)
  • Minimal technical logs (timestamps, errors)

Why We Collect It

  • To analyze traffic images for potential violations
  • To reply with results via WhatsApp
  • To improve reliability and troubleshoot issues

Processing and Storage

  • Images are fetched from WhatsApp Cloud API and processed by AI models
  • Analysis is performed in-memory; images are not persisted by default
  • Model outputs and logs may be temporarily stored to operate the service
  • Data is not sold

Sharing

  • We use service providers to run AI models and deliver messages (e.g., Google AI, Meta/WhatsApp)
  • We do not share your data with third parties for advertising

Retention

  • Images: ephemeral and discarded unless needed for debugging or legal compliance
  • Logs: retained up to 30 days for reliability and abuse prevention

Your Rights

  • Request access or deletion by contacting the owner
  • We verify ownership of the WhatsApp number before fulfilling requests

Security

  • HTTPS used for transport
  • Access to tokens and systems is restricted

Children

Not intended for use by children under the applicable age of consent.

Changes
