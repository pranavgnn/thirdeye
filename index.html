Traffic Violation Analyser — Prototype

University project demo. Not a real product.

Important: This is a university project prototype for research/learning purposes. Outputs may be inaccurate or incomplete and must not be used for real enforcement.

What it does

  • You send a traffic photo via WhatsApp.
  • An agentic AI pipeline analyzes the image, classifies potential violations, and drafts a report.
  • It decides whether automatic verification is confident or manual review is required.
  • You receive a short reply summarizing the findings.

How to try

  • Send a road-traffic image to the configured WhatsApp number (demo environment only).
  • Wait for the automated response summarizing detected violations (if any).

Data & Privacy

We process images only to generate the response. See our Privacy Policy for details.
